Welcome to the Preston Community Soup Kitchen

Our vision is to see no empty tummies. Adults, seniors and children have access to a daily meal. We need daily volunteers to help cook and serve a hot meal to community members.

We would also like to extend to a helping hand to low income families during the holidays that don't have acces to a holiday meal. We depend on the community for donations and manpower to put together holiday meal boxes. Also, a hot meal for those who prefer to come into the soup kitchen to enjoy a meal with the community.
